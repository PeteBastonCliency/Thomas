Building Air Flow Testing

To ensure uncontrolled air leakage is not coming in or out of the property is required through ‘Part L’ in the UK Building regulation. There is a need to demonstrate compliance which can be demonstrated through the air tightness testing.

Building Air Flow Testing across all Midlands

Air tightness tests of buildings are essential due to its impact on the building's EPC rating. These tests help ensure air does not escape or enter the property in uncontrolled ways whilst considering the need for adequate ventilation. Air tightness tests will help ensure the property's insulation is working properly and can affect its energy efficiency. Tom offers competitive pricing, a full report, including any potential issues.
